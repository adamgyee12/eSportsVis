The Numbers behind eSports

Looking at the growth and popularity of professional video gaming

What are eSports?

eSports have been gaining popularity over the past few years with the rise of streaming services such as Twitch.tv that allow viewers to tune into individual gamers, regular season matches, and even tournaments.

In the past 18 months, over 4 Billion hours of eSports were watched on Twitch.tv

eSports tournaments capture a global audience and are hosted in many counties all over the world.

Intel Extreme Masters Katowice 2017 was watched by 46 Million unique total viewers.
